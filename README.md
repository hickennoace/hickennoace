### 🧭 About me

I'm studying **Finance & Management**, and I'm genuinely fascinated by what numbers say when you ask them the right questions. Most of my projects start the same way: a real dataset, a question worth answering, and a hunt for the story (and the money) hidden inside it.

- 🔭 **Right now:** macro & markets analysis in Python, and dashboards that non-analysts can actually read.
- 🧮 **My lane:** financial analysis, exploratory data analysis, BI reporting, and statistics that hold up.
- 🎮 **On the side:** building a Unity (HDRP / C#) survival game with a small indie team — because shipping software keeps the analysis honest.
- 💬 **Ask me about:** correlation vs. causation, why a clean dashboard beats a clever one, and reading markets through data.
